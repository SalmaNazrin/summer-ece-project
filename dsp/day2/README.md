Applied Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) on a noisy mixed signal (5Hz + 50Hz sine waves) to recover the individual frequency components. Plotted the frequency spectrum and observed that both signal frequencies appear as distinct spikes above the noise floor, demonstrating FFT's ability to identify signals buried in noise.
Concepts covered: FFT, frequency domain, magnitude spectrum, noise floor, Nyquist theorem
Libraries used: NumPy, Matplotlib
